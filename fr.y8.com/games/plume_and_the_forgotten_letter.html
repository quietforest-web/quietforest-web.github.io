Détails concernant le jeu

Plume is a seven year old kid who has a dinosaur plush called Simon. She loves Christmas and getting gifts but this Christmas she forgot to send her letter to Santa. Now she wanted to go to Santa's place and give her letter by herself. Play this adventure game "Plume and the forgotten Letter". Get to Santa's place undetected and be as stealthy as you can. Collect all the keys so you can unlock Santa's room.
